BSP-NYC is a web application that provides New York City parents of special needs children with data and information. For more information about our services, view the video below.

Getting Started

  • Click Signup if you have not already registered.
  • Signin if you have previously registered an account on our site.
  • Once you are signed in, you can use the dropdown menu at the top of this page to edit your user profile and connect social accounts.
  • You can view all of our documents and e-learning modules by visiting the Products page.
  • Products you have purchased will show up in your Library.
